DC11331

DC11331, is a French association which is part of a set of DEFCON extending worldwide. It organizes monthly events concerning cyber security, in the form of a conference and a challenge. It also makes it possible to connect people from this milieu. The recognizable sign of DEFCON is the "smiley skull".
